Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start earning money on EarnBolt?
Getting started is easy! Download the app, sign up with Google, and you can immediately start earning by:
  • Watching video ads (earn 1-5 coins per ad)
  • Completing daily tasks (earn 10-50 coins)
  • Daily check-ins (earn streak bonuses)
  • Referring friends (earn 100 coins per referral)
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal amount is ₹10 (1000 coins). This low threshold ensures you can cash out your earnings quickly. Higher amounts like ₹50, ₹100, and ₹500 are also available for withdrawal.
How long does it take to process withdrawals?
Withdrawal processing times:
  • UPI/Bank Transfer: 1-3 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Gift Cards: 24-48 hours
You'll receive notifications about your withdrawal status updates.
How does the referral system work?
Earn money by inviting friends:
  • Immediate Bonus: 100 coins when someone signs up with your code
  • Activity Bonus: 10% of their earnings for their first month
  • Milestone Rewards: Extra bonuses when they reach certain earning levels
Share your unique referral code found in the "Refer & Earn" section.
Why are my ads not loading?
If ads aren't loading, try these solutions:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Close and restart the app
  • Clear app cache in device settings
  • Wait a few minutes - ad availability varies by region
  • Update to the latest app version
If issues persist, contact our support team.
How do I increase my earning level?
Your level increases based on total coins earned:
  • Level 1: 0-999 coins
  • Level 2: 1,000-4,999 coins
  • Level 3: 5,000-9,999 coins
  • Level 4: 10,000+ coins
Higher levels unlock better rewards and exclusive features!
Is EarnBolt safe and legitimate?
Yes! EarnBolt is completely safe and legitimate:
  • Secure Google authentication
  • Encrypted data storage with Firebase
  • Thousands of successful withdrawals processed
  • Transparent privacy policy and terms
  • Regular security audits and updates
We never ask for sensitive information like passwords or bank PINs.
Can I use EarnBolt on multiple devices?
Yes, you can use the same account on multiple devices by signing in with the same Google account. Your progress, coins, and settings will sync automatically across all devices.

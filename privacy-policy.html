Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use EarnBolt, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile information when you sign up with Google
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, ads watched, tasks completed, and earning activities
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Payment Information: UPI ID, bank details, or payment method details for withdrawals

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the EarnBolt service
  • Process your earnings and withdrawal requests
  • Send notifications about your account and earnings
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (payment processors, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard security
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only

5. Third-Party Services

EarnBolt uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Services: For authentication and analytics
  • Firebase: For app backend and data storage
  • Ad Networks: For serving advertisements (data may be shared with ad providers)
  • Payment Processors: For processing withdrawals

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

EarnBolt is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Sending you a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@earnbolt.in
  • Website: www.earnbolt.in
  • In-App Support: Available in EarnBolt app

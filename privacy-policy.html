Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025 | Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name and email address (when you register)
  • Phone number (for account verification)
  • Profile information (avatar selection from 18 available avatars, display name)
  • Google account information (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Payment details (UPI ID, Paytm mobile number, bank account details, IFSC codes)

1.2 Device Information

  • Device identifiers (Android ID, IMEI)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • IP address and general location information
  • Ad network identifiers for mediation

1.3 Usage Data

  • Watch & Earn Activities: Ad viewing count, daily watch count, booster activation status, ad completion rates
  • Referral System: Referral code generation, friend invitations, referral success tracking, milestone progress
  • Coin Economy: Coin balance, earning history, spending patterns, withdrawal requests
  • Leaderboard Data: Weekly watch-ads rankings, monthly referral rankings, bracket participation, reward eligibility
  • Daily Tasks: Check-in streaks, task completion status, bonus claims
  • Withdrawal History: Payment method preferences, transaction status, processing times

1.4 Firebase Data

  • Real-time database entries for user profiles, wallets, watch history, referrals
  • Cloud Functions execution logs for reward calculations
  • Analytics data for app performance optimization
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Push notification tokens for reward notifications
  • Authentication tokens and session management

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functions

  • Ad Mediation: Serve rewarded ads through Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and IronSource networks
  • Reward System: Calculate and distribute coins for ad watching (7-9 coins per ad with booster system)
  • Referral Program: Process referral codes, track friend sign-ups, distribute referral bonuses (50 coins instant + 300 coins on withdrawal)
  • Leaderboard Management: Rank users in weekly watch-ads and monthly referral competitions with bracket-based rewards
  • Wallet Operations: Manage coin balance, process withdrawal requests (₹10-₹500 via UPI/Paytm/Bank/Google Play)
  • Daily Tasks: Track check-in streaks, task completion, and bonus distributions
  • Deep Linking: Handle referral link clicks and app installations

2.2 Analytics and Improvement

  • Monitor ad performance across multiple networks for optimization
  • Track user engagement patterns to improve earning opportunities
  • Analyze withdrawal success rates and payment method preferences
  • Optimize leaderboard bracket assignments based on participation
  • Improve referral conversion rates and reward structures

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (payment processors, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard security
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Advertising Partners

Google AdMob (Primary Network)

  • Serves rewarded video ads for coin earning
  • Collects device identifiers, ad interaction data, and viewing patterns
  • Uses advertising ID for personalized ad delivery
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Unity Ads (Mediation Partner)

  • Provides additional rewarded video inventory
  • Integrated through Google Ad Manager mediation
  • Privacy Policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

IronSource (Mediation Partner)

  • Ad mediation and yield optimization
  • Helps maximize ad revenue and user rewards
  • Privacy Policy: IronSource Privacy Policy

5.2 Firebase Services (Core Infrastructure)

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In integration and user session management
  • Firestore Database: Real-time storage of user profiles, coin wallets, watch history, referral data, leaderboard rankings
  • Cloud Functions: Server-side processing for reward calculations, leaderboard updates, withdrawal processing
  • Analytics: App usage tracking, feature adoption rates, user retention metrics
  • Cloud Messaging: Push notifications for reward updates, leaderboard results, withdrawal status
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

8.1 Age Restrictions

  • Minimum Age: EarnBolt is intended for users 13 years and older
  • Earning Restrictions: Users under 18 may have withdrawal limitations based on local laws
  • Parental Consent: Required for users under 18 in certain jurisdictions
  • Data Protection: Enhanced privacy protections for users under 18
  • Automatic Deletion: Immediate removal of data if user is discovered to be under 13

8.2 Parental Controls

  • Account Monitoring: Parents can request information about their child's earning activities
  • Withdrawal Oversight: Parental approval may be required for withdrawals by minors
  • Usage Limits: Parents can request daily earning caps or time restrictions
  • Account Termination: Parents can request immediate account deletion for minors

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Sending you a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

10.1 Earning Data Usage

  • Fraud Prevention: We monitor earning patterns to prevent abuse and ensure fair rewards
  • Leaderboard Integrity: User activity is analyzed to maintain competitive fairness
  • Reward Optimization: Usage data helps improve earning opportunities and ad quality

  • Anonymous Sharing: Referral links contain codes, not personal information
